Research and Innovation is our core capability

    We believe in the power of technology as a tool to open minds and enlighten others. We leverage a variety of methods to help our customers explore and measure new ideas for creating business value.

Aircraft

    Our main workhorse for Thermography and Lidar measurements is the DJI Matrice 300 RTK with a H20T and L1 attachments.For visual imagery we use the DJI Mavic 2 Pro which offers 4k imagery for object detection and tracking

Remote Sensing

    We see life as a spectrum of energy and we offer unique capabilities to measure this energy using first principals and proven sensor technologies. Our clients benefit from our extensive experience with Sony CMOS image sensors, TDK InvenSense microphones, Texas Instruments PhotoDiodes and Pyreos Pyroelectric sensors.

Edge Computing

    We believe in the power of the Nvidia Jetson for measuring and quantifying our work at the edge. We have also found value in coupling the Raspberry Pi and Google Coral TPU for low-power remote sensing applications.

Our Partners

    Our Additive Manufacturing and EMS Service partners allow us to transform our proof-of-concepts into minimal viable products that can scale to meet the needs of local municipalities, small businesses and large corporations.
